Rules

Team game: Group 1 vs Group 2 using net scores.

Par 4s: count the best 1 net ball from each group.

Par 5s: count the best 2 net balls from each group.

Par 3s: count the best 3 net balls from each group.

Each group can win the front, back, and overall. Lowest cumulative net team score wins each segment.

Skins: lowest individual net score on a hole wins a skin. Ties cut the skin.
